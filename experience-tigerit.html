Technical Support Assistant

CAAB ICT & E-Governance ERP Project

Project Overview

Worked on a large-scale, mission-critical Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP / MIS) system for the Civil Aviation Authority of Bangladesh (CAAB), deployed across all civil airports and CAAB operational units nationwide.

The system is hosted on on-premise infrastructure within CAAB’s secured data center and supports multi-departmental operational, financial, administrative, and regulatory workflows.

Application Support & Ops

  • Served as a core member and acting lead of the Application Support Team, providing L1–L2 application support for CAAB’s enterprise ERP system used by government officials across multiple airports.
  • Acted as the primary coordination point between end users, QA, developers, database, and network teams to ensure uninterrupted service availability and issue resolution.
  • Supported and monitored production ERP modules including Security Administration, Accounting, HR, Billing & Revenue, and Air Traffic Services.

QA & Testing

  • Conducted Regression Testing, Functional Testing, and User Acceptance Testing (UAT) for new features, enhancements, and bug fixes prior to production deployment.
  • Verified business logic, data integrity, access control, and workflow accuracy across modules aligned with government compliance and operational requirements.
  • Collaborated closely with QA and Development teams to validate fixes and ensure stable releases.

Team Leadership

  • Functioned as the de facto Application Support Team Lead, guiding team members in issue triage, prioritization, and resolution.
  • Allocated tasks, supervised daily ticket handling, and ensured SLA-driven response for routine L1 incidents.
  • Coordinated on-site and remote support activities for CAAB officials, including desk support and system walkthroughs.

User Training

  • Conducted end-user training programs for CAAB officials at multiple airports, covering ERP workflows, module usage, and operational best practices.
  • Delivered remote training and live support sessions using remote access tools for geographically distributed users.
  • Assisted in improving user adoption and reducing operational errors through structured guidance.

Bug Tracking & Docs

  • Collected, analyzed, and documented software bugs and system issues reported by end users across airports.
  • Prepared structured bug reports, reproduction steps, impact analysis, and validation notes for QA & development teams.
  • Performed user requirement gathering and functional specification inputs for new modules.

Crisis Management

  • Participated in critical system outage recovery scenarios, providing leadership and coordination during emergency service disruptions.
  • Worked remotely with network and infrastructure teams to restore ERP services during unexpected server/data center outages.
